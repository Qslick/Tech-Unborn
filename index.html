TechUnborn is our very ambitious project to bring new advancements  in science, and technology onto the center stage through podcasting, storytelling, and social media. In todays world, it is necessary to stay informed about how our digital lives are changing if we hope to maintain the freedoms that we all currently enjoy. It is however infeasible to ask everyone to search through long press releases and submitted patients to try and stay informed. At TechUnborn we strive to keep you up to date about what our future might look like in an entertaining, and simplistic way, which is why we can't wait to release TechUnborn to you!
